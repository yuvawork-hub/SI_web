Features

Everything Your Business Needs

A complete billing solution built for small shops and wholesale businesses.

Instant Bill Generation

Create professional bills in seconds. Select products, add customer — bill ready. Share as PDF via WhatsApp instantly.

Bluetooth Thermal Print

Print directly to any 80mm Bluetooth thermal printer. Save printer address — one click print next time.

Sales Dashboard

Track today's sales, 7-day performance, retail vs wholesale split. Visual bar charts to grow your business.

Product Management

Add products with images, set retail & wholesale prices, organize by categories. Quick search built-in.

Customer Management

Save customer details, search by name or phone, select with one tap when creating bills.

Store Customization

Add your store name, address, phone number. Show your brand on every printed bill. Tamil & English support.
